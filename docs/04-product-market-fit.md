# Product–Market Fit

## PMF definition for Kairos

Kairos reaches product–market fit when the right kind of person, in the right kind of decision, feels that they should not move forward without first going through this process.

This is stronger than:

- “this sounds interesting”
- “I would try it”
- “this could help”

The real PMF threshold is:

“I need this before I decide.”

## First inevitable user

The first user for whom Kairos becomes inevitable is:

a believer with a real spiritual life, currently facing a concrete decision, who has already prayed or reflected, but still has not been able to close and act.

This person:

- is not spiritually indifferent
- is not just curious
- is not looking for generic content
- is stuck between perceived direction and concrete obedience

## First exact use case

The first use case defined for Kairos is:

deciding whether to continue or end a romantic relationship.

This case was chosen because it contains:

- high emotional load
- real spiritual seriousness
- fear, attachment, guilt, and ambiguity
- urgency without triviality

If Kairos can carry a person through this case with integrity, it proves strength under pressure.

## Early market signal

An initial WhatsApp survey produced:

- 14 yes
- 3 depends
- 3 no

This does not prove PMF, but it does show early resonance around the problem and the proposed form of help.

## Core PMF hypothesis

People will pay for structured spiritual discernment if:

- the decision is important
- the process feels serious and trustworthy
- the system clearly helps them move from confusion to closure

## Commercial implication

Kairos is not primarily a subscription product.

Its natural commercial unit is one decision session.

The user buys clarity for a decision they have not been able to close.

## PMF milestone to watch

The strongest sign of PMF is not initial interest.

It is recurrence:

- the user comes back with another decision
- the user recommends the experience to others
- the user or a leader wants multiple codes for a community
