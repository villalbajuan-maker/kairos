# Project Overview

## Name

`Kairos`

Kairos refers to the opportune moment, the decisive moment, the moment charged with meaning. For this project, the name points to the moment in which a person must discern and act before God with clarity.

## Core idea

Kairos is not a devotional app, not a generic Christian chatbot, and not a passive content platform.

Kairos is a guided discernment process for important decisions.

Its purpose is to help a person move from ambiguity to a concrete, obedient, executable decision through:

- structured reflection
- intentional prayer
- guided interaction with Scripture
- reduction of ambiguity
- explicit decision and action

Kairos is also conceived as a bilingual product from its foundation.

Its primary operating languages are:

- English
- Spanish

## What Kairos is not

Kairos does not replace:

- God
- the Holy Spirit
- prayer
- Scripture
- pastoral counsel

Kairos does not decide for the person.

Kairos does not claim authority over the person’s conscience.

Kairos does not exist to generate spiritual content for consumption.

## What Kairos does

Kairos introduces order, sequence, discipline, and structure into the moment of decision.

Its role is to guide the human process of discernment so that a person can:

- define the real decision
- separate emotion from conviction
- seek God intentionally
- reduce inner ambiguity
- close in a concrete decision
- execute the decision in reality

## Foundational thesis

The problem is not that God does not speak.

The problem is that many people do not have a clear, disciplined process to receive, order, discern, confirm, and obey the direction they already believe God is giving them.

Kairos exists to address that gap.
