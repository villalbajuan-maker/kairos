# Landing Messaging Framework

## Purpose

This document translates the Kairos narrative into a landing-page messaging structure.

It is not yet a visual design.

It is the messaging architecture that should guide:

- landing page copy
- first public positioning
- short-form external communication
- conversion-oriented product explanation

## Strategic goal of the landing

The landing should do five things clearly:

1. Explain what Kairos is.
2. Make the problem feel real and recognizable.
3. Show why Kairos is different.
4. Make the process feel trustworthy and serious.
5. Move the user toward starting one decision.

## Core rule

The landing must not sound like:

- a church website
- a generic Christian app
- a vague self-help product
- a generic AI wrapper

It must feel like:

a serious decision-guidance product for important moments before God.

## Overall narrative arc

The landing should move through this emotional and conceptual sequence:

- this is for a real moment in my life
- this understands my problem
- this is different from generic advice or content
- this can help me move toward clarity
- I can try this on one decision now

## Recommended landing structure

1. Hero
2. Problem recognition
3. Why Kairos exists
4. How it works
5. Why it is different
6. Trust and boundaries
7. Call to action

## 1. Hero section

### Objective

Explain Kairos in one glance and create immediate relevance.

### Hero headline options

#### Option 1

Discern clearly. Decide faithfully.

#### Option 2

When the decision matters, clarity matters.

#### Option 3

Turn spiritual clarity into a real next step.

### Hero subheadline options

#### Option 1

Kairos is a guided process for important decisions before God.

#### Option 2

Move from ambiguity to a clear and executable next step through structured discernment, prayer, and reflection.

#### Option 3

Kairos helps you organize what you believe God is showing you until it becomes a real decision.

### Hero CTA options

- Start one decision
- Bring your decision
- Begin with one decision
- Find clarity now

### Hero support line

One decision at a time. One clear next step.

## 2. Problem recognition section

### Objective

Help the user feel seen in the exact tension Kairos addresses.

### Core message

Many people already pray, reflect, and seek guidance, but still remain stuck when the decision is in front of them.

### Section headline options

- You do not always need more input. Sometimes you need more clarity.
- The hardest part is not always hearing. It is deciding.
- Some decisions stay open too long.

### Supporting copy

You may already be praying.
You may already be searching Scripture.
You may already have asked for advice.

And still, the decision remains open.

Kairos exists for that moment.

### Recognition bullets

- You know this decision matters.
- You have thought about it again and again.
- You feel the tension between clarity, fear, and action.
- You do not want noise. You want a real next step.

## 3. Why Kairos exists

### Objective

Introduce the category and the reason the product exists.

### Core message

Kairos was built for the moment when a person needs help moving from spiritual ambiguity to concrete action.

### Section headline options

- A new category for important decisions.
- More than content. More than advice.
- Built for the moment of decision.

### Supporting copy

Kairos is not a devotional app and not a generic AI assistant.

It is a guided discernment experience for people who want to make important decisions with clarity before God.

## 4. How it works

### Objective

Reduce mystery and make the experience feel practical and trustworthy.

### Core message

Kairos guides you through a structured process that helps you move from confusion to action.

### Section headline options

- How Kairos works
- A guided path from ambiguity to action
- One clear process for one important decision

### Recommended step copy

#### Step 1

Define the real decision.

#### Step 2

Separate what you feel, want, and fear.

#### Step 3

Seek clarity intentionally through prayer and reflection.

#### Step 4

Reduce ambiguity and recognize what is becoming clear.

#### Step 5

Commit to a concrete next step.

#### Step 6

Follow through.

### Supporting line

Kairos does not decide for you. It helps you move through the process with structure and clarity.

## 5. Why it is different

### Objective

Show why Kairos is not interchangeable with AI chat, content, or generic coaching.

### Core message

Kairos is different because it is designed around discernment, not distraction.

### Section headline options

- Why Kairos is different
- Not another Christian app
- Built for decision, not drift

### Comparison language

Most products give you content.

Kairos gives you a process.

Most AI tools generate responses.

Kairos guides discernment.

Most reflective tools help you think.

Kairos helps you close.

### Pillars

- One decision at a time
- Structured clarity, not open-ended chat
- Spiritual seriousness with clear boundaries
- Action, not just reflection

## 6. Trust and boundaries

### Objective

Increase trust by clearly naming what Kairos is and is not.

### Core message

Kairos uses technology for what technology does well, while preserving what belongs to God and wise human guidance.

### Section headline options

- Built with spiritual seriousness
- Technology has a role. And a boundary.
- Structured guidance without false authority

### Supporting copy

Kairos does not replace God, the Holy Spirit, Scripture, or trusted human counsel.

It helps organize the process around a decision so that you can move toward clarity and action with integrity.

### Optional trust bullets

- Guided, not controlling
- Structured, not mechanical
- Serious, not sensational
- Helpful, not spiritually presumptuous

## 7. CTA section

### Objective

Move the user into one decision session.

### Core message

Do not sell the entire future.

Invite one clear beginning.

### CTA headline options

- Bring one decision.
- Start with the decision that is still open.
- Begin with what matters now.

### CTA supporting copy

You do not need to have clarity yet.

You only need to begin with one real decision.

### CTA button options

- Start one decision
- Begin now
- Find clarity
- Bring your decision

## Recommended page tone

The landing should feel:

- clean
- calm
- serious
- contemporary
- spiritually grounded without becoming religiously noisy

## Messaging hierarchy

If the user remembers only five things, they should remember:

1. Kairos helps with important decisions.
2. It is for people who want clarity before God.
3. It is not generic AI or generic Christian content.
4. It guides a real process.
5. It starts with one decision.

## Short-form reusable versions

### One-sentence version

Kairos is a guided discernment experience that helps people turn spiritual clarity into real decisions.

### Two-sentence version

Kairos helps you move from ambiguity to a clear next step before God. It combines structured guidance, reflection, and spiritual seriousness to help you close important decisions.

### Short pitch version

Kairos is building a new category around guided spiritual discernment for important decisions.

## Conversion reminder

The landing should not try to prove everything.

Its job is to create enough trust, recognition, and clarity that the user says:

“Yes, this is for the decision I am carrying right now.”
